This is the HomePage of the IBM PERCS ProgrammingModel Wiki.

We will be using it for discussing X10, the programming model for the PERCS project.

The outline of the language is as follows

* XtenOverview and basic concepts. 
** Example program: JacobiIteration

* ScalarClasses
** ReferenceClasses
** ValueClasses
* XtenArrays
** XtenRegions
** XtenDistributions
* XtenClocks
* XtenStatements
* XtenExpressions
* ExecutionEnvironment
