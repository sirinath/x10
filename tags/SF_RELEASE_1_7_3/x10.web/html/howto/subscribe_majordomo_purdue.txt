HOWTO: Subscribe to the X10 mailing lists
----------------------------------------

Send a message to majordomo@cs.purdue.edu with an empty 
subject and the following text in the message body:

   subscribe x10-dev
   subscribe x10-lang
   end

Of course you can choose which of these lists to subscribe to. 
x10-dev is meant primarily for a discussion of the implementations,
and x10-lang is meant primarily for language discussions.

Jan Vitek will approve and you will receive a confirmation 
message that your subscription has been approved.
