Operations on distributions
§ D.region ::= source region of distribution
§ D.rank ::= rank of D.region
§ D | R ::= region restriction for distribution D and region R (returns
a restricted distribution)
§ D | P ::= place restriction for distribution D and place P (returns
region mapped by D to place P)
§ D1 || D2 ::= union of distributions D1 and D2 (assumes that
D1.region and D2.region are disjoint)
§ D1.overlay(D2) ::= asymmetric union of D2 over D1
§ D.contains(p) ::= true iff D.region contains point p
§ D1 – D2 ::= distribution difference: D1 | (D1.region – D2.region)
